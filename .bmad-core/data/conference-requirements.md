# Conference Requirements Reference

## Purpose

Quick reference for major data science conference requirements and formatting guidelines.

## Major ML/AI Conferences

### NeurIPS (Neural Information Processing Systems)

- **Page Limit**: 9 pages main + unlimited appendix/references
- **Format**: NeurIPS LaTeX style
- **Blind Review**: Yes (double-blind)
- **Abstract**: 250 words max
- **Supplementary**: Allowed (reviewers not required to read)
- **Special Requirements**:
  - Broader Impact Statement
  - Reproducibility Checklist
  - Code submission encouraged

### ICML (International Conference on Machine Learning)

- **Page Limit**: 9 pages main + unlimited appendix/references
- **Format**: ICML LaTeX style
- **Blind Review**: Yes (double-blind)
- **Abstract**: 250 words max
- **Supplementary**: Allowed
- **Special Requirements**:
  - Ethics Statement (if applicable)
  - Reproducibility Section

### ICLR (International Conference on Learning Representations)

- **Page Limit**: 9 pages main + unlimited appendix/references
- **Format**: ICLR LaTeX style
- **Blind Review**: Yes (double-blind)
- **Abstract**: 250 words max
- **Supplementary**: Allowed
- **Special Requirements**:
  - Open review process
  - Code submission strongly encouraged

### AAAI (Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence)

- **Page Limit**: 7 pages + 1 page references
- **Format**: AAAI LaTeX/Word style
- **Blind Review**: Yes (double-blind)
- **Abstract**: 150 words max
- **Supplementary**: Limited (2 pages)
- **Special Requirements**:
  - Ethical Impact Statement

### CVPR/ICCV/ECCV (Computer Vision Conferences)

- **Page Limit**: 8 pages + references
- **Format**: CVF LaTeX style
- **Blind Review**: Yes (double-blind)
- **Abstract**: 150-200 words
- **Supplementary**: Allowed
- **Special Requirements**:
  - Video submissions encouraged
  - Reproducibility checklist

## Data Science Specific

- **Format**: ACM double-column
- **Blind Review**: Yes (double-blind)
- **Abstract**: 200 words max

- **Special Requirements**:
  - Reproducibility Section
  - Data Availability Statement

- **Format**: ACM double-column
- **Blind Review**: Yes (double-blind)
- **Abstract**: 150 words max
- **Special Requirements**:
  - Ethical Considerations

### WSDM (Web Search and Data Mining)

- **Page Limit**: 9 pages + references
- **Format**: ACM double-column
- **Blind Review**: Yes (double-blind)
- **Abstract**: 200 words max

## Common Requirements Across Conferences

### Formatting Rules

- **Font Size**: Usually 10pt or 11pt
- **Margins**: Specific to template (don't modify)
- **Columns**: Single or double (conference-specific)
- **Line Spacing**: Template-defined
- **Page Numbers**: Usually suppressed for submission

### Anonymization Rules

- Remove author names and affiliations
- Remove acknowledgments
- Anonymize self-citations ("Our previous work [?]")
- Remove funding information
- Avoid identifying details in text

### Citation Formats

- **NeurIPS/ICML/ICLR**: Author-year style
- **ACM Conferences**: Numbered references
- **IEEE Format**: Numbered in brackets [1]

### Figure Requirements

- High resolution (300 dpi minimum)
- Vector graphics preferred
- Readable when printed B&W
- Captions below figures
- Tables caption above

### Submission Formats

- **Primary**: PDF (usually PDF/A)
- **Source**: LaTeX strongly preferred
- **Supplementary**: ZIP file
- **Code**: GitHub link or ZIP

## Special Sections

### Reproducibility Statement

Should include:

- Dataset availability and preprocessing
- Complete hyperparameter settings
- Random seeds used
- Computational requirements
- Code availability

### Broader Impact Statement

Should address:

- Positive applications
- Potential negative uses
- Affected stakeholders
- Mitigation strategies
- Societal implications

### Ethics Statement

Required when:

- Using human subjects data
- Potential for misuse
- Fairness/bias concerns
- Privacy implications
- Environmental impact

## Submission Timeline Tips

- **Abstract Deadline**: Usually 1 week before paper
- **Paper Deadline**: Strict (no extensions)
- **Supplementary**: Same as paper deadline
- **Rebuttal**: 1-2 weeks after reviews
- **Camera-Ready**: 1 month after acceptance

## Common Rejection Reasons

- Over page limit
- Poor anonymization
- Missing related work
- Insufficient evaluation
- Overclaimed results
- Poor clarity/organization
- Not novel enough
- Missing ablations
